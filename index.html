Shop Friction Free

Customers can start shopping by tapping their smartphones against specially-coded NFC stickers embedded in price labels on Electronic Shelf Labels (ESL), shelf edges or even on a product itself. Reduce Queues

JusTap removes the need for having physical tills in a store, allowing customers to quickly complete their purchase at any point. This gives you an opportunity to manage the additional space created and allows your staff to spend more time on the shop floor ready to help your customers with any queries.
