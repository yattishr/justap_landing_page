Reduce Queues

JusTap removes the need for having physical tills in a store, allowing customers to quickly complete their purchase at any point. This gives you an opportunity to manage the additional space created and allows your staff to spend more time on the shop floor ready to help your customers with any queries.

tap.

tap your mobile device once on a bar-coded label or an NFC sticker

scan.

scan the product information and our intelligent virtual assistant will provide you with the product information, allergen information (if any) and also recommend similar products that may be of interest to you.

pay.

you can add the product that you have chosen directly into your virtual shopping cart. once your product is in the virtual cart, you can pay for the item directly from your mobile device while standing in the aisle in-store. no need to wait in long queues anymore to pay for your items.
